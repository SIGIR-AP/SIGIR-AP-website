STUDENT TRAVEL GRANT

SIGIR-AP 2024 SPECIAL REGISTRATION FOR STUDENT TRAVEL GRANT PROGRAM APPLICANTS


SIGIR-AP 2024 offers limited time registration fees exclusively for applicants to the SIGIR Student Travel Grant Program, which provides funding for the awardees to help cover the cost of SIGIR-AP 2024 registration.

  • To take the benefit, the applicants have to register through the dedicated page mentioned below by November 15, 2024, AoE (November 16, 21:00 JST), winner or not.
  • After the above deadline, this page will be closed and they will have to register through the generic registration process.


    • REGISTRATION FEES


    SIGIR-AP 2024 provides special registration discounts for the following type of registration for the SIGIR Student Travel Grant Program applicants.

  • 4-Day Passport: Full access to all conference events and services, including the tutorials and workshops (with lunches on Days 2 and 3, a banquet, and a student dinner if eligible).
    • SIGIR-AP 2024 provides limited time registration discounts depending on your status as follows.
  • Student travel grant winner: you can receive the 4-Day Passport at no cost.
  • Student travel grant applicants (non-winner): you can receive an early registration discount.
    • The lists of registration fees are as follows.

    Early Bird (From now to November 15, 2024, AoE (November 16, 21:00 JST))

    Registration type 4-Day Passport
    In-person Student Travel Grant Winner JPY 짜0
    Student Travel Grant Applicant (Non-Winner) JPY 짜35,000
    Online Student Travel Grant Winner JPY 짜0
    Student Travel Grant Applicant (Non-Winner) JPY 짜20,000
    After the above deadline, you will have to register through the generic registration process and pay the late registration fee in order to attend the conference, winner or not.

    REGISTRATION PROCESS


    For information other than the ones described below, please see REGISTRATION PROCESS section on the generic Registration page.
    Registration:
  • If you wish to take advantage of the discount, you have to register through the limited time Payvent* webpage called Special Registration for Student Travel Grant Applicants, by November15 AoE (November 16, 21:00 JST).
    • *Payvent is an online registration and payment system.

    Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation requests received before November 15, 2024, AoE (November 16, 21:00 JST) will be eligible for a refund, after deducting a 6% cancellation fee. However, please note that at least one author of each accepted paper is required to register and present the paper at the conference (either remotely or in person); hence, requests of cancellation will not be accepted unless another co-author is registered for the event.
  • No refund will be issued for cancellation after November 15, 2024, AoE (November 16, 21:00 JST).
  • If you want to switch your in-person registration to virtual, please also contact the SIGIR-AP 2024 local organisers before November 15, 2024, AoE (November 16, 21:00 JST). However, this is also subject to a 6% cancellation charge.
