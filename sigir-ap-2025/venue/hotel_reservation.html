Wyndham Grand Xi'an South

The Wyndham Grand Xi'an South is perfectly positioned in the heart of the Tang Dynasty Ever-bright City, Qujiang New District. The hotel is adjacent to the Tang Dynasty City Wall Ruins Park and is just a 10-minute walk from prominent attractions such as the Big Wild Goose Pagoda, Tang Paradise, and Qujiang Pool. Additionally, it is conveniently close to Metro Line 4, providing easy access to transportation.

Booking Notice

  • Booking Deadline: Attendees must complete their reservations by December 1, 2025.
  • For International Attendees: Full payment for accommodation must be made online at the time of booking. Unpaid reservations will be automatically canceled.
  • For Chinese Attendees: A charge for two nights' accommodation is required as a "room reservation fee" online. This fee will be refunded to the original payment card within 5 working days after the conference. However, the full accommodation charge must also be paid at the hotel front desk upon arrival.

Refund Policy

Invoice/Receipt

  • Chinese Participants: Accommodation invoices can be obtained at the hotel front desk upon check-out. For twin rooms, separate invoices can be issued upon request.
