Registration

REGISTRATION FOR SIGIR-AP 2023 IS NOW OPEN!

SIGIR-AP 2023 is committed to fostering international collaboration and ensuring accessibility for participants from around the world. To this end, we have streamlined the registration process to accommodate both international and domestic attendees, offering the convenience of paying in two widely accepted currencies: USD (United States Dollar) for international registration and RMB (Chinese Renminbi) for domestic registration.

For our international participants, registering for SIGIR-AP 2023 is a straightforward process. Using the ACM Registration Vendor, we accept registration payments in USD. This approach ensures ease of registration for delegates from various countries and minimizes currency exchange complications. People who need Chinese VISA to attend the conference must pay through the international registration portal in order to receive VISA support from ACM.

At the same time, we recognize the importance of accommodating our domestic attendees from within China. To ensure accessibility for our local participants, we are pleased to offer the option of registering in RMB. People who cannot pay in USD or need invoices in Chinese must pay through the domestic registration portal.

SIGIR-AP 2023 is committed to ensuring accessibility for everyone, including those who may not be able to attend the conference in person. Recognizing the importance of virtual participation in today's global landscape, we are pleased to offer virtual registration as well. People who only attend the conference virtually can receive a discount for the registration (described in details below). Also, as a part of SIGIR-AP's effort to be carbon neutral, we encourage attendees to purchase optional carbon credits for their attendance of the conference.

Each person attending the conference must pay a registration fee. To offset the full fee, a range of discounts are being offered, including for students, for ACM members, and for online-only attendees. The presenter of each accepted paper must be registered for conference as described in the previous sentence, and is expected to pay an additional fee associated with each paper that they are presenting. One conference attendee may be the presenter of multiple papers if they wish, provided that they are an author of each presented work, and provided that they pay the additional fee associated with each paper. For example, an author who (1) wants to attend the full conference in person, (2) registers before the early-bird deadline, (3) is a student, (4) is an ACM member, and (5) pays the publication fee for her or his paper, needs to pay

Cancellations

  • Requests received before the early-bird deadline (10/15/2023) will be eligible for a full refund, except for the publication fee.
  • If you want to transfer your in-person registration to virtual, it has to happen in the early-bird period (before 10/15/2023).
  • No refund will be issued for cancellation after the early-bird deadline (10/15/2023).
  • At least one author of each accepted paper is required to attend and present the paper in the conference, i.e., request of cancellation will thus not be honored unless another co-author is registered for the event.
  • Requests for registration cancellation must be made in writing and emailed to the SIGIR-AP 2023 Registration Chair. All refunds are likely to be processed after the end of the event and issued back through your payment method.
